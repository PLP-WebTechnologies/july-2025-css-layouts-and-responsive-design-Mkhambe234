Why I Love Granny Squares

Granny squares are timeless, versatile, and beginner-friendly. They let you use up leftover yarn and create blankets, bags, or even clothing. Each square feels like a tiny finished project!

3 Tips for Choosing Yarn

Start with soft, medium-weight yarn—it’s forgiving for beginners. Lighter colors make stitches easier to see. And don’t be afraid to experiment with textures once you’re confident!

Crochet & Mindfulness

Repetitive stitches can calm the mind. Crochet helps you slow down, focus, and find joy in small progress. It’s more than a hobby—it’s a mindful practice of patience and creativity.

My First Crochet Blanket

I started with simple double crochet rows. Mistakes became part of the charm, and finishing it gave me confidence. Crochet is about progress, not perfection!

Stitch of the Week

Half Double Crochet: The perfect stitch between single and double—great for cozy scarves!
